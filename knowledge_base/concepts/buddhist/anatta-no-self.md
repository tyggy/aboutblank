# Anatta (Non-Self)

## Definition

Buddhist doctrine that the self is illusory, constructed from impermanent causal factors rather than a single enduring entity

## Category

- Primary: [[Buddhist]]

## Alternative Names

Also known as: "No-Self", "Doctrine of Non-Self", "Selflessness", "Non-self", "No Self"
