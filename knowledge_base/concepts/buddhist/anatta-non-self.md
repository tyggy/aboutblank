# Anatta (Non-Self)

## Definition

Buddhist philosophical doctrine that persons are not unified, enduring entities but aggregates of impermanent factors

## Category

- Primary: [[Buddhist]]

## Alternative Names

Also known as: "No-Self", "Doctrine of Non-Self"
