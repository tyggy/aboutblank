# Emptiness

## Definition

Buddhist philosophical concept that everything lacks inherent independent existence and depends on other phenomena; enables stress-free knowledge of all things.

## Category

- Primary: [[Buddhist]]

## Alternative Names

Also known as: "Paramartha", "Shunya", "Śūnyatā"
