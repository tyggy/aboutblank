# Mind Blindness

## Definition

Evolutionary limitation causing humans to be hyperfixated on three-dimensional space and medium-sized objects; prevents recognition of other forms of intelligence.

## Category

- Primary: [[Cognitive]]

## Alternative Names

Also known as: "Anthropocentric Bias"
